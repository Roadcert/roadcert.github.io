Earned Recognition.

Designed by transport managers for transport managers

“Earned Recognition” is a new initiative by the DVSA that is linked to your Operator Compliance Risk Score system. Transport operators who make their regulatory compliance documents available for the DVSA to view online and providing that they meet with the required standards their vehicles are less likely to roadside stops therefore freeing up valuable DVSA resources to tackle non-compliant and rogue operators.
